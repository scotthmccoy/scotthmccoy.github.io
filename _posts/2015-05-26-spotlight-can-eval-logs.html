Used it to calculate the length of a mega-dose of LSD (well, more like a kilo-dose):

Since ln(foo) / ln(2) = log base 2 of foo,

Additional Half-Lives endured by taking a dose 1024 times stronger than usual = ln(1024)/ln(2) = 10

A half-life is about 4 hours, so you're looking at a good +40 hours on your trip.
